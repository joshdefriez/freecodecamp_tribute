Liang Shuming

Early Twentieth-century Confucian Reformer

paragraph about Liang Shuming

Timeline

  • Born October 18, 1893 in Guilin City, Guangxi Province, Qing Empire
  • Recruited in 1917 by Cam Yuanpei to study philosophy at Beijing University
  • 1921, published “Eastern and Western Cultures and their Philosophies,” which established Liang’s theory of three cultures
  • 1931-1937, instructed rural reconstruction in Zouping County, Shandong Province, Republic of China
  • While in Zouping, Liang trained 800 people to run schools across the province
