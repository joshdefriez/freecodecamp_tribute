Liang Shuming

Early Twentieth-century Confucian Reformer

Liang Shuming was an important early and mid-twenieth century Chinese reformer and philosopher.

Timeline

  • 1893 , born in Guilin City, Guangxi Province, Qing Empire
  • 1917, recruited by Cam Yuanpei to study philosophy at Peking University
  • 1921, published “Eastern and Western Cultures and their Philosophies,” which established Liang’s theory of three cultures
  • 1931-1937, instructed rural reconstruction in Zouping County, Shandong Province, Republic of China
  • 1931-1937, While in Zouping, Liang trained 800 people to run schools across the province
